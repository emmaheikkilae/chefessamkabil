About Chef Essam

From the officers' table to the world's finest kitchens

Essam's path started with engineering and commerce studies in Egypt — then a season serving French Foreign Legion officers in French Guiana changed everything. What followed was formal French culinary training, his own restaurant in Montpellier, and nearly four decades across five-star hotels on five continents.
