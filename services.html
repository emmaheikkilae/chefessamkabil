Services

Four ways to work with Chef Essam

Whichever format fits your occasion, every menu draws on the same foundation: classical French technique, Mediterranean and Middle Eastern flavor, and genuine dietary flexibility.

01 — Catering

For celebrations, weddings & corporate events

Who it's for: Private parties, weddings, corporate events, milestone celebrations.

Formats: Plated dinners, buffet stations, passed hors d'oeuvres, themed tasting menus.

Example offerings:

  • French Bistro Night — coq au vin, tarte tatin, curated wine pairings
  • Mediterranean Table — mezze spread, grilled seafood, herb-forward vegetables
  • Egyptian–French Fusion Tasting Menu
Request a Catering Quote
02 — Cooking Lessons

Learn his technique, hands-on

Who it's for: Individuals, couples, small groups, corporate team-building.

Formats: In-home private lessons, small-group classes, themed workshops.

Example offerings:

  • Hands-on crêpe-making — his certified specialty
  • Classic French mother sauces
  • Mediterranean mezze night
Book a Cooking Lesson
03 — Private Chef

A chef for your home, on your schedule

Who it's for: Families, small groups, dinner parties, extended stays.

Formats: Single-event private dinners, multi-day stays for vacation rentals or family gatherings.

Example offerings: Custom weekly menus, holiday dinners, dietary-specific meal plans.

04 — Multi-Day Meal Prep

One big session, meals for days

Who it's for: Busy families, new parents, anyone who wants home-cooked meals on hand without cooking every night.

Formats: One extended cooking session that produces multiple portioned meals, ready to reheat over the days ahead. Cooked in your kitchen, or in Essam's kitchen and delivered.

Example offerings:

  • A week of French-Mediterranean weeknight dinners, portioned per person
  • Vegetarian or dietary-specific batch cooking
  • New-parent meal support — heat-and-eat comfort food for the first weeks home
